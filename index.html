SHE'S SO PERFECT

I genuinely enjoy your presence,

the way speaking to you steadies the world.

You make my days feel lighter

simply by being unapologetically you.

When my spirit sinks, you pull laughter out of me;

when joy finds me, you're often the reason why.


You’re silly yet strangely mature,

intelligent in ways you brush off with jokes.

Loud when you’re wild, soft when you speak,

gentle by nature but strong in will

you never take nonsense from anyone.

A little chaotic, a little crazy,

yet somehow still cute, still kind,

still a mystery stitched with warmth.


I’m grateful for the privilege

of knowing you these past two years,

grateful for how you’ve become

woven into my life as I have into yours.

Your voice is nurturing, grounding,

your eyes paint stories I never tire of seeing

a picture that stays bright,

even on my dim days.


And when darkness presses heavy on my chest,

your voice becomes the calm my heart can hear;

you turn my restless thoughts into quiet air,

and pull me back from fears I cannot name.


Your presence is a light I never sought,

yet somehow it guides the way my days unfold.

You lift the corners of my tired thoughts,

and warm the parts of me that once felt cold.

You’re wild, yet wise; unfiltered, yet so kind

a perfect storm of chaos, heart, and grace.

Your strength is stitched in every word you find,

your softness lives in every look, every trace.

For all you are , your truth, your spark, your flame

I’m grateful, deeply moved,

to simply speak your name.
